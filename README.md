# Alchemize Calculator

A native desktop app designed for participants of **Alchemize**. This calculator helps you log project hours, track reward goals, and optimize your currency conversions to squeeze the absolute maximum "Potion Mix" out of your grind.

## Features

* **Project Logging & Inventory:** Log the hours you spend building projects across the three seasonal themes (Indie Gamedev, No Internet, Endless). Automatically tracks your base yield of Redstone, Glowstone, and Aqua Regia.
* **Trade Portal Optimizer:** Don't waste hours grinding inefficiently. The optimization algorithm crunches the event's mixing bonus rules to give you the exact breakdown needed to hit a target Potion Mix. It outputs multiple strategies:
  * *Max Bonus (1:1 Mix):* The absolute most efficient path (+1 Mix/hr bonus).
  * *Balanced Path:* Spreads hours across all three themes while keeping time investment low.
  * *Pure Speed:* The fastest brute-force path.
* **Grant Calculator:** Input a monetary target (e.g., a $100 Phone Grant) and the item's cost, and the app will tell you exactly how many hours you need to grind including an Optimized Min. Grind calculation.
* **Goal Tracking:** Set a specific currency target and date range and a visual progress bar keeps you updated on whether you are ahead of or falling behind your required pace.

# Theme: No Internet

The project follows No Internet theme by having a native desktop app, doesn't require any APIs, data is saved locally, and all the assets are are bundled directly inside the executable file.
